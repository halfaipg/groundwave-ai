AI Chat Tips

Keep it short: Due to LoRa bandwidth limits, shorter questions get better responses. The AI is trained to be concise.

Direct messages work best: Send a DM to the bot node for the best AI experience. Channel messages may have limited responses.

Clear history: Use {{ command_prefix }}clear to start a fresh conversation.

FAQ

How do I join the mesh network?

Get a Meshtastic-compatible device (T-Deck, T-Beam, Heltec, etc.), flash Meshtastic firmware, and your device will automatically discover nearby nodes.

What's the range of the mesh?

Individual LoRa links can reach several kilometers line-of-sight. Through mesh hopping, the network can extend much further as nodes relay messages.

Do I need internet to use this?

No! The mesh network operates independently. Your device communicates directly with other nodes via radio. The bot node has internet for AI and weather, but you don't need it.

Why are responses sometimes slow?

LoRa is designed for long range, not speed. Messages are sent at low data rates to maximize reliability. The AI also takes a moment to generate responses.
